Frequently Asked Questions
How do I accept an order? ›

Go to the "New Orders" tab. Tap the green ACCEPT button on any order card. The order moves to your "Active" tab and is locked to you — no other agent can take it.

How is my delivery earning calculated? ›

Each order shows the earning (₹) before you accept. Urgent orders include a bonus. Earnings are automatically added once you tap Mark as Delivered.

What if the customer doesn't pick up? ›

Try calling 3 times. If no response, wait 5 minutes at the drop location, then contact customer care at 1800-123-4567. Do not leave the package unattended.

How and when do I get paid? ›

Earnings are credited to your UPI account every Monday for the previous week. COD amounts must be deposited at the QuickDrop hub within 24 hours.

How do I update my bank/UPI details? ›

Visit the hub in person with a valid ID and passbook, or email support@quickdrop.in with your agent ID and new account details. Changes take 2–3 business days.

What if an order item is damaged? ›

Do not deliver damaged goods. Photograph the item immediately and call the support line. Never return items to the shop without supervisor approval.

How do I get a higher rating? ›

Be punctual, polite, and careful with items. Deliver within estimated time and confirm the customer's name before handing over. A 4.5+ rating qualifies you for priority orders.

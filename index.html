OUR STORY

A LEGACY BUILT ON CONCRETE VALUES

Founded in 1982 in Annavarm, Andhra Pradesh, Gopikiran Construction has grown from a small family enterprise into one of the most trusted construction companies in the region. For over four decades, we have shaped skylines, built communities, and earned the confidence of thousands of clients.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and timely delivery has made us the preferred partner for residential developments, commercial complexes, government road projects, and precision steel fabrication across AP and beyond.

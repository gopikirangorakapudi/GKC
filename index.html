FAQ
How do I accept an order?›

Tap the green Accept slider on any new order card. Once accepted, the order moves to your Active tab and is locked to you only.

What are the 6 delivery steps?›

1) Accept Order → 2) Order Picked Up → 3) Out for Delivery → 4) Money Received → 5) Enter Customer OTP → 6) Mark as Delivered.

What is the COD timer?›

For Cash on Delivery orders, you have 5 hours to complete the delivery. If the timer expires, the order is flagged. The timer is visible on the order tracking page.

How and when do I get paid?›

Earnings are credited to your UPI every Monday for the previous week. COD must be deposited at the GoCart hub within 24 hours.

What if the customer doesn't respond?›

Call 3 times, wait 5 minutes at drop location, then contact support at 1800-123-4567. Never leave packages unattended.
